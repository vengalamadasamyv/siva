Founded in 1997 by Dr. S. Sivanaryanan and Late Dr. S. Subashini as an OPD center, Siva Hospital grew through years of dedicated service and community trust. By 2003, it expanded into a 25-bedded hospital, with Paediatrics and Obstetrics & Gynaecology as its primary specialties. Over time, the hospital became a recognized center for high-risk paediatric care, supported by a fully equipped NICU. In 2023, Dr. S. Sanjay and Dr. N. Athmika joined the institution, continuing its legacy of quality, compassionate healthcare.
